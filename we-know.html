We Know What You Are Hiding (2017), for Vogue Italia

Photographer: Viktor Naumovski

Designer: Rachael Cheong

Assistant: Esperanza de Vries

Models: Sebastiaan Schoen, Sjors Beerendonk, Mari Janeva, Huilin Shi and Florence Marceau-lafleur


Vogue Italia

The photo series We Know What You Are Hiding is a collaboration between photographer Viktor Naumovski and fashion designer Rachael Cheong. The collection relates to disguising your deepest, darkest, secrets through the eyes of a porcelain doll. In this photo series, both creatives introduce the viewer to the body of the doll as a medium of hiding, in a rather twisted and deviant manner. The deformation of the body and referenced elements of classical horror are packed in an uncanny colorful narrative.
