Viktor Naumovski
Joker Makes Shooting Star (2016)

Based on true events. In the year 1910, the Halley Comet was passing closer than ever to planet Earth. In panic, a religious group from Oklahoma known as 'Secret Followers' organized a special ritual in which they would sacrifice a virgin, thus preventing the end of the world. With this project, I play with the boundaries between fact and fiction combining staged photography with found/scientific visual and theoretical documents related to the event itself.
